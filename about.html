AboutMe

I have been doing quality furniture reupholstering, and repair full time for over 40 years. I was lucky to grow up in my father’s custom reupholstering shop. I learned well from masters like my father and his workers who had been with him for an average 40 years

My father learned the trade in Brockton MA at Eureka Upholstery and was offered to open his own shop in West Haven CT which he and his two first cousins Les and Harold did for about 50 years.

They had bought small cottages in the Cape Cod area which I summered at growing up. When they retired there in 1979 I decided to open our shop there as well and moved with them to the Cape. I ran the shop there until 2005 at which point I moved to NH having been taken in by it’s beauty and afforded lifestyle.
